   **Answer:** The two most recognized certifications are the Certified ScrumMaster (CSM) from Scrum Alliance and the Professional Scrum Master (PSM) from Scrum.org. These certifications validate your knowledge and ability to apply Scrum practices.

   **Answer:** A Scrum Master's responsibilities include facilitating daily Scrum meetings, removing roadblocks, ensuring the team follows Scrum principles, helping with Sprint planning, and improving team dynamics.

   **Answer:** The Scrum Master helps maintain the integrity of the Scrum process, ensures that the team works collaboratively, removes obstacles that could delay progress, and fosters continuous improvement.
